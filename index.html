About Telkhola Government Primary School

Telkhola Government Primary School is a longstanding institution dedicated to providing quality education to young minds. Situated in a vibrant community, the school has been a cornerstone of learning and development for children, fostering a nurturing environment where students grow both academically and socially. With a focus on foundational education, the school emphasizes not only academic excellence but also the importance of character development, teamwork, and creativity. The dedicated teachers at Telkhola Government Primary School are committed to shaping future leaders by encouraging curiosity, critical thinking, and a love for learning.
